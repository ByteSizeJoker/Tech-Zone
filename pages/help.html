Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

We collected some of the most common questions so that you can find answers faster. If your question is not here, you can always contact our customer service.

  1. What is the return policy?
    We give 30-day return policy. If you are not happy with what you buy, you can return it in 30 days and get full refund.

  2. How do I track my order?
    You can login to your account and check under “Order History” tab. If you are confused, just ask customer service and they will help.

  3. What forms of payment do you accept?
    We take all common credit cards like Visa, Mastercard and American Express. You can also use PayPal.

  4. How do I reset my password?
    On login page click “Forgot Password” link. Then follow the steps on screen to make new password.
